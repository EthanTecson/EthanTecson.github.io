Visualize Your Budgeting

Shift Owl budgets your available balance using the 50/30/20 method. 50% to needs, 30% to wants, and 20% to savings. Additionally, the app can visually track what areas of life your money is going.

Create Your Own Budget and Set Goals

Want to get more specific than the 50/30/20 budgeting strategy? Feel free to create your own budget categories and decide your split percentage. Looking to hit a certain money goal? Shift Owl has got you covered!

Track Your Transactions and Export to Excel

Have access to all past transactions according to date and even export it all to excel if you please.
